Activity 1: Iot Apps

What is an Iot Application

IoT apps use machine learning to process sensor data in the cloud, enabling real-time monitoring through dashboards and notifications. Key features include security, connectivity, automation, and real-time data.

What are the main Features needed?

Good Quality Sensors, Security, Real-time Data, Connectivity, Integrity, Automation

What are some Examples?

Escalators, Automatic Taps, Smart Doors, Pacemakers, Alexa & Google Assistant, Healthcare System

Activity 2: Smart Building

What does Smart mean

Smart buildings use IoT technology to collect and analyze data from connected devices, such as lighting, HVAC, security, and energy management systems. This enables automation and control, improving efficiency, comfort, safety, and sustainability. The result is a more intelligent and responsive building environment.

What Features needed?

Sensors, Building Management system, Artificial Intelligence, Controlled Appliances, Security and Surveillance

What are Smart-Building Sceanarios

Corporate business building: Smoke detectors, automatic water sprinklers(Prevent fire breakout),Automatic lightning (To save electricity),Facial recognition (attendance of employees)

Activity 3: Controll Inferface

What are the high-level features?

An IoT control interface allows users to interact with connected devices and systems to monitor, manage, and control them. Examples include web-based dashboards, mobile apps, voice assistants, and physical control panels. It serves as a bridge between the user and the IoT infrastructure, enabling access to data and control over behavior.

What are the parts needed?

Camera (CMOS sensor), Fingerprint sensor( Capacitive sensors), Azure App service, Azure SignalR service, Fire alarm

Who are the users?

Employees, Clients, Recognised and unrecognised workers
